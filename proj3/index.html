Acrylic Video

For the final project I chose to do some video manipulation. I really like the look acrylic makes when stacked on top of each other, creating another image. Video clips from BBC Motion Gallery.

I interlaced video clips with stock footage to make a mosaic of interesting images. The main reason for this program was to explore video manipulation. I really enjoyed the bit mashing of the different images and found some really interesting techniques along the way.

Due to the nature of this sketch, it is better to download a pre-made, stand-alone application and run natively on your computer. Please select your computer's architecture:

  • Linux 32bit
  • Linux 64bit
  • Mac OSX 32bit
  • Mac OSX 64bit
  • Windows 32bit
